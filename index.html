About Us

Atma Ram Sanatan Dharma College an eminent college of the University of Delhi, stands out in its idiosyncratic way of teaching, providing quality education to the students and inculcating life skills in the classroom and beyond. Catering to the needs of students and society, A.R.S.D provides co-curricular opportunities to the students to develop an integrated personality on academic as well as social front. The pedagogy aims to instil socially responsibilites in students and is influenced by the philosophy of balance and the need to develop all facets of one’s personality in equal measure.
